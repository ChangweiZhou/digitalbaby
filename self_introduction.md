# About the Developer

Hello! I am a researcher and software engineer passionate about the intersection of **Neuroscience-inspired AI** and **Efficient Reinforcement Learning**. 

My work focuses on developing compact yet powerful architectures that can reason about complex environments without the need for billions of parameters. **Genesis V66** is part of my ongoing "Liquid Brain" series, where I experiment with:

- **Dynamic Plasticity:** Using Hebbian-inspired rules for local learning.
- **Sparse Routing:** Optimizing computation through Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) and group-based routing.
- **Pure Reasoning:** Challenging models to develop internal logic purely through outcome-based reinforcement (R1-Zero philosophy).

I believe that the future of AI lies in **efficiency, interpretability, and emergent logic**.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Deep Learning:** PyTorch, Transformers, Reinforcement Learning (PPO, GRPO).
- **Architectures:** MoE, Neural Plasticity, State-Space Models.
- **Tools:** Python, WandB, Hugging Face.
